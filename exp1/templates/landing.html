Exploration of Word Size Data Encodings

Description of Research

You are invited to participate in a research study about factors influencing perception of texts. De-identified data may be shared with collaborators at other universities. The study will take about 5 minutes.

To participate, you must:

  • HAVE NOT ALREADY COMPLETED THIS STUDY
  • be between 18-65 years of age.
  • have normal or corrected to normal vision
  • be comfortable with reading text through online interface
  • have JavaScript enabled in your browser.

What Will My Participation Involve?

You will be presented with groups of texts, and then answer questions about their comparative sizes.
